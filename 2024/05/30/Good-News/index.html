Good News

To follow up on my last post, yesterday I decided to start updating some things on this website. I also mentioned my frustration about my study permit for Canada taking so long to process, which is preventing me from completing my graduate program. Originally, I planned to document my reapplication to a graduate program as a blog series, but this morning, my application tracker showed that my background check is complete. Hopefully, this means I’ll be able to get my visa in the near future, something I’ve been waiting for two years and am very excited about!

If I do get it, I’ll probably update some posts here about my studies and life at the University of Ottawa (this might really turn into a diary ;). I really hope this wish comes true.
